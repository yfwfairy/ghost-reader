# Ghost Reader Manual Checklist

- Launch the app and confirm only one window is created.
- Resize the main window narrower and wider; confirm it stops at the minimum width.
- Click `Pin` and confirm the main window stays above other apps.
- Open a TXT book and confirm the app navigates to the reader page in-place.
- Click `Back to bookshelf` and confirm the app returns to the bookshelf.
- Open an EPUB book and confirm the content stays stable instead of flashing.
- Reopen the same book and confirm progress resumes.
